About 

NCS is Central Ohio’s first and leading dedicated Roof Restoration Company. NCS is a MFG certified applicator of specialty roof coatings manufactured and warranted by GAF materials, ER Systems and Republic Powdered Metals. Warranties range from 10-20 years, material and labor (MFG warranty to owner direct). We inspect with mfg rep and install for the following substrates: METAL, EPDM, BUILT-UP, AND SINGLE-PLY.

NCS performs these services on projects from 10,000 SF to over 300,000 SF.
